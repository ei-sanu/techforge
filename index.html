FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can participate in TechForge?

TechForge is open to students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts from across North India. Whether you're a student, a budding entrepreneur, or a coding prodigy, you're welcome to join!

What's the team size requirement?

Teams can consist of 2-4 members. Individual participants can also register, and we'll help match you with a team based on skills and interests.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Bring your laptop, charger, any specific hardware you might need for your project, and your enthusiasm! Food, beverages, and workspaces will be provided at the venue.

Are there any prizes?

Yes! TechForge offers substantial cash prizes for winners, opportunities for internships and job placements, and special recognition from our sponsors. Detailed prize information will be announced soon.

Can I start working on my project before the hackathon?

No, all projects must be started from scratch at the beginning of the hackathon. However, you can brainstorm ideas beforehand. All code must be written during the event duration.
